Sparse-BitNet: 1.58-bit LLMs are Naturally Friendly to Semi-Structured Sparsity

Di Zhang, Xun Wu, Shaohan Huang, Yudong Wang, Hanyong Shao, Yingbo Hao, Zewen Chi, Li Dong, Ting Song, Yan Xia, Zhifang Sui, Furu Wei 8.6/10 arXiv PDF

摘要

半结构化 N:M 稀疏性和低比特量化（例如 1.58-bit BitNet）是提高大语言模型（LLM）效率的两种有前景的方法，但它们大多被孤立地研究。在这项工作中，我们研究了它们的相互作用，并表明 1.58-bit BitNet 天然比全精度模型更兼容 N:M 稀疏性。为了研究这种效应，我们提出了 Sparse-BitNet，一个统一框架，联合应用 1.58-bit 量化和动态 N:M 稀疏化，同时首次确保稳定训练。在多个模型规模和训练训练机制（稀疏预训练和密集到稀疏调度）中，1.58-bit BitNet 在相同稀疏水平下始终表现出比全精度基线更小的性能下降，并且可以在精度崩溃前容忍更高的结构化稀疏性。此外，使用我们的自定义稀疏张量核心，Sparse-BitNet 在训练和推理中都实现了实质性的加速，高达 1.30 倍。这些结果突出了将极低比特量化与半结构化 N:M 稀疏性相结合是高效 LLM 的有前景方向。

原文

Semi-structured N:M sparsity and low-bit quantization (e.g., 1.58-bit BitNet) are two promising approaches for improving the efficiency of large language models (LLMs), yet they have largely been studied in isolation. In this work, we investigate their interaction and show that 1.58-bit BitNet is naturally more compatible with N:M sparsity than full-precision models. To study this effect, we propose Sparse-BitNet, a unified框架 that jointly applies 1.58-bit quantization and dynamic N:M sparsification while ensuring stable training for the first time. Across multiple model scales and training regimes (sparse pretraining and dense-to-sparse schedules), 1.58-bit BitNet consistently exhibits smaller performance degradation than full-precision baselines at the same sparsity levels, and can tolerate higher structured sparsity before accuracy collapse. Moreover, using our custom sparse tensor core, Sparse-BitNet achieves substantial speedups in both training and inference, reaching up to 1.30X. These results highlight that combining extremely low-bit quantization with semi-structured N:M sparsity is a promising direction for efficient LLMs.

研究背景与动机

领域现状

两种效率优化路径： 1. 极端量化：1.58-bit BitNet（1.58 比特量化） 2. 稀疏化：半结构化 N:M 稀疏性（如 2:4）

研究空白： - 两种方法被孤立研究 - 未探索它们的相互作用

研究动机

核心发现：1.58-bit BitNet 与 N:M 稀疏性天然更兼容！

创新点与方法

创新1：联合优化 - 功能：同时优化量化和稀疏化 - 发现：1.58-bit BitNet 在相同稀疏水平下性能下降更小 - 意义：打破量化和稀疏化的研究壁垒

创新2：稀疏张量核 - 功能：自定义硬件加速器 - 效果：训练和推理加速 1.30x

创新3：稳定训练 - 挑战：极低比特 + 稀疏化训练不稳定 - 方案：首次实现稳定训练
