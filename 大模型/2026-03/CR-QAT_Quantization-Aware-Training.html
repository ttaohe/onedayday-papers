CR-QAT: Curriculum Relational Quantization-Aware Training for Open-Vocabulary Object Detection

Jinyeong Park, Donghwa Kim, Brent ByungHoon Kang, Hyeongboo Baek, Jibum Kim 7.8/10 arXiv ↗ PDF ↗
📄 摘要
开放词汇目标检测（OVOD）通过视觉-语言对齐实现新类别检测，但巨大的模型尺寸阻碍了在资源受限设备上的部署。虽然量化提供了实用的压缩，但我们揭示了朴素极端低比特（如 4-bit）量化严重破坏细粒度视觉-语言对齐并扭曲区域间关系结构。为了解决这个问题，我们提出了课程关系量化感知训练（CR-QAT），一个结合逐步优化与关系知识蒸馏的集成框架。在 CR-QAT 中，我们设计了关系保护正则化来在量化期间保护区域间关系，并实现了课程学习策略，使模型逐步适应量化同时保留关系知识。
原文

Open-vocabulary object detection (OVOD) enables novel category detection via vision-language alignment, but massive model sizes hinder deployment on resource-constrained devices. While quantization offers practical compression, we reveal that naive extreme low-bit (e.g., 4-bit) quantization severely degrades fine-grained vision-language alignment and distorts inter-region relational structures. To address this, we propose curriculum relational quantization-aware training (CR-QAT), an integrated framework combining stage-by-stage optimization with relational knowledge distillation. Within CR-QAT, we design relation-preserving regularization to protect inter-region relationships during quantization, and implement a curriculum learning strategy that gradually adapts the model to quantization while preserving relational knowledge.

🔍 研究问题与背景
  • 研究背景：OVOD 模型太大，需要压缩部署
  • 研究动机：极端低比特量化破坏视觉-语言对齐和区域关系
  • 核心方法：CR-QAT - 课程关系量化感知训练
  • 主要结果：在 4-bit 量化下保持 OVOD 性能
  • 研究意义：首次研究量化对 V-L 对齐的影响
