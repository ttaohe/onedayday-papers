摘要翻译

英文摘要

Mixture of Experts (MoE) models enable parameter-efficient scaling through sparse expert activations, yet optimizing their inference and memory costs remains challenging due to limited understanding of their specialization behavior. We present a systematic analysis of expert specialization in MoEs through two complementary approaches: domain-specific routing patterns and an early decoding framework that tracks expert contributions to output representations. Our analysis of the DeepSeekMoE model reveals that despite having 64 routed experts with 6 active for each layer's computation, the model exhibits significant expert specialization redundancy - a subset of experts consistently dominate computations across layers. Based on these insights, we propose expert pruning strategies that reduce active experts by 50% while maintaining performance.

中文翻译

混合专家（MoE）模型通过稀疏专家激活实现参数高效扩展，但由于对其特化行为的理解有限，优化其推理和内存成本仍然具有挑战性。我们通过两种互补方法系统分析 MoE 中的专家特化：领域特定的路由模式和跟踪专家对输出表示贡献的早期解码框架。我们对 DeepSeekMoE 模型的分析揭示，尽管每层计算有 64 个路由专家和 6 个活动专家，模型表现出显著的专家特化冗余——一个专家子集在各层中持续主导计算。基于这些洞察，我们提出了专家剪枝策略，在保持性能的同时将活动专家减少 50%。
